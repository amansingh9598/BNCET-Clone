Director Message

We believe that skill development has to be at the core of engineering because the more we give importance to skill development, the more competent will be our youth and competency leads to innovation, productivity, and overall growth. The institution has always endeavored to provide skill-based education and quality technical education to the students through our skillful, motivated, and highly qualified faculties to develop an environment of academic excellence and innovation. To keep pace with the dynamic changing world, we prepare students to be global citizens.

The institute provides academic and personal counseling to each student on a regular basis.

May it be individual or institute, each has its own character, its own distinct identity. But what stands out is ‘Knowledge’ with ‘Moral values.’ It is this philosophy we are following in our institute.
